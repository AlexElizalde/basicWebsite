About the gucci birb

The common raven (Corvus corax) is found freaking everywhere! It's notable for using tools for gathering food and recreation. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.

Features

The common raven is known for its sleek black feathers. They can live to around 21 years. They eat whatever they want and have adapted to increasing urbanization.
